01 / The problem

Finding the right groove should not interrupt the song.

Most MIDI libraries contain hundreds or thousands of files.

Finding the right groove often takes longer than writing the song.

These packs are designed around songwriting outcomes rather than endless folders of MIDI files.

02 / The approach

Built for songwriters
